Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026 · Last updated: March 1, 2026

Summary: CareerTracker collects only what's necessary to provide you the service. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is stored securely in Supabase (hosted on AWS).

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use CareerTracker, we collect:

  • Username — your chosen identifier (no email required)
  • Password — stored as a SHA-256 hash, never in plaintext
  • Display Name — the name shown in the interface
  • Profile Photo — if you choose to upload one (stored in Supabase Storage)
  • Application Data — job applications, stages, notes, and schedules you create
  • Contact Messages — messages you send to admin, bug reports, and feature requests
  • Privacy Consent Record — timestamp of when you accepted this policy

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your personal dashboard
  • To store and sync your job application records across devices
  • To respond to your support messages, bug reports, and feature requests
  • To display your profile photo and display name within the app

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • SHA-256 password hashing — passwords are never stored in plaintext
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) — database-level access controls ensure users only access their own data
  • HTTPS encryption — all data in transit is encrypted via TLS
  • Supabase infrastructure — hosted on AWS with enterprise-grade security

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from your Profile Settings, which will permanently remove all associated data within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

CareerTracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and file storage (supabase.com)
  • Google Fonts — typography (no personal data shared)

No advertising networks, tracking pixels, or analytics services are used.

6. Your Rights

  • Access — you can view all your data from your dashboard
  • Correction — update your display name and photo in Profile Settings
  • Deletion — delete your account and all data from Profile Settings
  • Export — contact admin to request a data export

7. Children's Privacy

CareerTracker is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes to this policy. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Use the Email Admin page within the app.
